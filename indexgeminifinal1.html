The Artistic Journey

"Dance is the hidden language of the soul."

My journey into the world of rhythm and expression began at the tender age of three. What started as a childhood fascination has blossomed into a lifelong spiritual pursuit.

With over 350 performances across diverse platforms, I have had the privilege of sharing this ancient art form on prestigious stages, from auspicious temples to national television (Doordarshan).
